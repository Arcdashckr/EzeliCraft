## 🎮 MINECRAFT MOD LISTESI (Parça 5) 🎮

### 🦁 Mobs (Yaratıklar / Canlılar)
🔹 **[[EMF] Entity Model Features](https://modrinth.com/mod/entity-model-features)**
> 📝 EMF is an, OptiFine format, Custom Entity Model replacement mod available for Fabric and Forge.

🔹 **[Deeper and Darker](https://modrinth.com/mod/deeperdarker)**
> 📝 A Minecraft mod which features more blocks, items, armors, and hidden mysteries to complement the Deep Dark biome.

🔹 **[Naturalist](https://modrinth.com/mod/naturalist)**
> 📝 Adds 24 animals *(not including variants!)* to your world with immersive behaviors and drops.

🔹 **[Creeper Overhaul](https://modrinth.com/mod/creeper-overhaul)**
> 📝 A mod which overhauls the vanilla creepers!

🔹 **[Enderman Overhaul](https://modrinth.com/mod/enderman-overhaul)**
> 📝 Enderman Overhaul adds over 20 new enderman variants, each with their own sounds, models, and animations!

🔹 **[Just Enough Breeding (JEBr)](https://modrinth.com/mod/justenoughbreeding)**
> 📝 JEI/REI/EMI plugin that displays breeding information

🔹 **[Animal Feeding Trough](https://modrinth.com/mod/animal_feeding_trough)**
> 📝 Mobs self feed with feeding trough

🔹 **[YUNG's Cave Biomes](https://modrinth.com/mod/yungs-cave-biomes)**
> 📝 Adds new vanilla+ cave biomes and underground content!

🔹 **[[ESF] Entity Sound Features](https://modrinth.com/mod/esf)**
> 📝 Allows for entity sound variation using OptiFine format property rules

🔹 **[Zombie Horse Spawn](https://modrinth.com/mod/zombie-horse-spawn)**
> 📝 🧟‍♂🐴 Allows zombie horses to spawn with a zombie riding it and other tweaks.

🔹 **[Skeleton Horse Spawn](https://modrinth.com/mod/skeleton-horse-spawn)**
> 📝 ☠🐴 Allows skeleton horses to spawn naturally with a skeleton riding it and other tweaks.

🔹 **[Villager In A Bucket](https://modrinth.com/mod/villager-in-a-bucket)**
> 📝 Pick up a villager in a bucket!

🔹 **[Golem Overhaul](https://modrinth.com/mod/golem-overhaul)**
> 📝 Golem Overhaul adds 9 new golem types, all with their own unique mechanics!

🔹 **[Random Mob Sizes](https://modrinth.com/mod/random-mob-sizes)**
> 📝 configure mobs to spawn in different sizes

🔹 **[Ghost](https://modrinth.com/mod/ghost)**
> 📝 Adds little ghosts to Minecraft :D

🔹 **[All Loot Drops](https://modrinth.com/mod/all-loot-drops)**
> 📝 👝 Living entities drop all their possible loot in a configurable amount, looting enchant supported.
