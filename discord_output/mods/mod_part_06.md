## 🎮 MINECRAFT MOD LISTESI (Parça 6) 🎮

### 🍎 Food (Yiyecek)
🔹 **[AppleSkin](https://modrinth.com/mod/appleskin)**
> 📝 Food/hunger-related HUD improvements

🔹 **[Smarter Farmers (farmers replant)](https://modrinth.com/mod/smarter-farmers-farmers-replant)**
> 📝 Allows villagers to replant the correct seed & allows them to use modded ones

🔹 **[Nature's Delight](https://modrinth.com/mod/natures-delight)**
> 📝 A mod which integrates Nature's Spirit and Farmer's Delight Refabricated.

🔹 **[Nutritious Milk](https://modrinth.com/mod/nutritious-milk)**
> 📝 🥛 Adds nutritious value to milk, filling up the food bar and saturation when consumed.
