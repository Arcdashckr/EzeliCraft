## 🎮 MINECRAFT MOD LISTESI (Parça 10) 🎮

### 🎮 Diğer / Sınıflandırılamayan Modlar
🔹 **[MixinTrace](https://modrinth.com/mod/mixintrace)**
> 📝 Adds a list of mixins in the stack trace to crash reports

🔹 **[Bobby](https://modrinth.com/mod/bobby)**
> 📝 Allows for render distances greater than the server's view-distance

🔹 **[Artifacts](https://modrinth.com/mod/artifacts)**
> 📝 Adds various treasure items that can be found through exploration

🔹 **[Repurposed Structures - Fabric](https://modrinth.com/mod/repurposed-structures-fabric)**
> 📝 Adds more variations of vanilla structures and features such as a Jungle Fortress!

🔹 **[Universal Ores](https://modrinth.com/mod/universal_ores)**
> 📝 Adds ores variants for andesite, diorite, granite and tuff

🔹 **[Tom's Trading Network](https://modrinth.com/mod/toms-trading-network)**
> 📝 A simple mod to make trading with players easier

🔹 **[Dungeons+](https://modrinth.com/mod/dungeons+)**
> 📝 Brand new vanilla dungeons remade with biome specific designs and new mob spawner types

🔹 **[Cycle Paintings](https://modrinth.com/mod/cycle-paintings)**
> 📝 🖼️ Easily cycle through placed paintings by right-clicking them with another painting in hand.

🔹 **[Fast Villager Curing](https://modrinth.com/mod/fast-villager-curing)**
> 📝 Villagers cure in 1 tick instead of 3 to 5 minutes.
